WELCOME TO KSRL INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Ever since the blossoming of our first KSRL in Hyderabad in 2002, to a chain of 90+ International Schools, we have impacted more than 75,000 lives, setting a new benchmark for quality of education. KSRL The International School is one of the top International Schools, blooming all over Aurangabad, Bengaluru, Bhopal, Chennai, Delhi, Gurgaon, Hyderabad, Indore, Jabalpur, Jaipur, Kolkata, Mumbai, Nagpur, Pune, Rohtak, Sonipat, Tumkur.

Along with a focus on overall growth, Orchids ensure students safety and security with round the clock monitoring of school premises through dedicated supervisors and high definition CCTV cameras. As one of the best International CBSE schools in Mumbai it is our responsibility to make sure our students feel safe and comfortable not only while studying but also while they are busy with sports and creativity.
